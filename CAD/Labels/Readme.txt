*** Screw Terminal Label Instructions ***

---Materials---
Printer: Brother PT-P900W
Label tape: TZe-S135 (12mm/0.47", White on clear, strong adhesive)

---Print settings---
Print resolution: Standard
Output: Standard
Options: Half Cut, Output Directly to Printer Driver. ALL OTHERS UNCHECKED

---Instructions---
Print labels
Cut to size on dotted lines, excluding the lines.
Peel and stick on label space above wire entry holes on wire terminal block.




*** Lickstick Logo Decal Instructions ***
Print with a die-cut sticker service.
Recommended vendor: Jukebox Print, https://www.jukeboxprint.com/ select Permanent Matte finish.
